Is this for you?

You'll recognise your team in these signals

  • 01 You have 1–4 designers and no dedicated design manager or head of design
  • 02 Design reports to a CPO, VP Product, or founder who's stretched thin
  • 03 Your designers are capable, but you sense they're not reaching their potential
  • 04 You've had unexpected attrition, or designers telling you they don't feel supported
  • 05 You want to retain and grow your design team without a full-time leadership hire yet

Your designers are capable.
But are they growing?

At Series A and B, design teams often work hard and ship well — but without someone focused on their development, they plateau.

About me

I'm a senior designer with a decade of experience across Fintech, SaaS and AI, and a background in cognitive psychology that shapes everything about how I work with people and teams.

Over the last several years I've been mentoring designers independently — from career changers finding their first role to senior designers navigating a plateau. Seeing those people land jobs, grow in confidence, and find their direction is what convinced me to formalise this as a service.

I think about design systemically: why people get stuck, what actually builds confidence, and how team dynamics shape individual performance.
